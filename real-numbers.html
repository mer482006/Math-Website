REAL NUMBERS

Natural Numbers start from 1,2,3 and so on till ∞. Whole numbers are natural numbers except that these set of numbers start from zero. Integers are basically negative numbers and positive natural numbers where zero is considered neither. Rational Numbers are integers in the form of p/q where q ≠ zero. If it was equal to zero, then the result would be infinity, or in mathematical terms, it would be undefined. And the opposite of rational numbers also exist, they are called irrational numbers. In simpler terms, irrational numbers are numbers which cannot be written in the form of p/q where q ≠ zero. The set of rational and irrational numbers is called as real numbers. Also, there are imaginary numbers, which cannot be represented on the cartesian plane. Examples of such imaginary numbers would include 5i, 9i, 39i, where i is the unit of imaginary numbers.


Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic

It states that every composite number can be factorised as a product of its primes. For
example:

  • 24 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 = 23 3 1
  • 356 = 2 × 89 = 21891
  • 2025 = 3 × 3 × 3 × 3 × 5 × 5 = 3 4 5 2

If a number can be represented in the form of 2m5n, it will end with the digit zero.

HCF AND LCM

HCF = Product of smallest power of each common
prime factor in the numbers

LCM = Product of largest power of each prime
factor involved in the numbers

HCF (a,b) × LCM (a,b) = a × b




Proof of irrational numbers.

To prove that the given number is irrational, the first step would be
assuming that its a rational number. In the second step, we do the
calculations, and derive that our assumption was wrong. So, in this manner,
we can prove that the given number is irrational. This method of prooving
numbers is called proof by contradiction.

Decimal expansions of Rational Numbers

Decimal expansions of rational numbers can be terminating,
non-terminating recurring and non-terminating non-recurring.
A rational number p/q where q isn't in the form of 2m5n, will be
non-terminating recurring. When q is in the form of 2m5n, the
number will have terminating decimal expansion.
